Subtitle Delayer

Reads frames directly from the page <video> element.
No screen capture needed. No flicker. No feedback loop.

Step 1: Install or Test

⏱ Subtitle Delayer

Drag the button to your bookmarks bar  |  Or click "Test on this page" to verify it works first

iPad / can't drag: Copy the URL from the box below and paste it into a bookmark's URL field manually.

Step 2: How to use

  1. Open a video page and start playing
  2. Click Subtitle Delayer in the bookmarks bar
  3. Drag the blue box over the subtitle area. Drag corner to resize
  4. Adjust the delay slider. Wait a few seconds for warm-up
  5. Click the bookmark again to close
If you see an error alert after clicking, copy the message and share it for debugging.
